04. Layout Principles

Kinetic Ledger Soft

Our philosophy combines the rigidity of high-end data management (The Ledger) with a kinetic, approachability (The Soft). Every grid is deliberate, every spacing value is a multiple of 4, yet the visuals feel fluid and alive.

grid_view

Asymmetrical Balance

Avoid symmetrical grids; lead the eye with large typography balanced by dense technical UI blocks.
